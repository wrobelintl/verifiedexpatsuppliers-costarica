Can Americans and Canadians own property in Costa Rica?

Yes — Costa Rica has some of the most foreigner-friendly property ownership laws in Latin America. Foreigners have the same property rights as Costa Rican citizens. No restrictions on foreign ownership.

What is the Pensionado Visa in Costa Rica?

The Pensionado (Retiree) visa requires proof of at least $1,000/month in pension income. It grants residency with significant benefits including import duty exemptions and access to the public healthcare system (CAJA).
